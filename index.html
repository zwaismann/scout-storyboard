Live action storyboards by
Ze'ev Waismann & Electra Filmworks

01

Inside the garage, looking toward the open door. The Traveler faces the morning light. The father prepares the gear while the daughter heads to the car. The journey begins.

02

His hand unplugs the EV charger. Backlit and deliberate. A quiet domestic ritual before hitting the road.

03

The daughter in the passenger seat, chin tilted toward the glass. The landscape blurs past and she's already somewhere else. Our first real look at her.

04

Father and daughter framed from the center console. A shared silence. The road unspools on the navigation screen between them.

05

The Traveler idles at a weathered gas station, alone in a vast golden landscape. Old world meets new.

06

His hand on the old gas pump, a familiar gesture. The same motion as the charger at home - only the world around him has changed.

07

The Traveler speeding across an open dirt trail, kicking up dust through the golden hills. The vehicle in its element.

08

Low angle as the Traveler crests a ridge, dirt falling away beneath it. Built for exactly this.

09

Inside the cabin. She's laughing now, bracing against the door. He catches it in the corner of his eye and grins.

10

The Traveler at rest on an uneven dirt trail, the desert stretching out in every direction. Nowhere to be but here.

11

A breathtaking vista. Father and daughter standing beside the parked Traveler, gazing out over a massive, rolling valley bathed in late afternoon light.

12

Close on her face against the sunset. Wind in her hair. She's seeing something for the first time and she knows it.

13

Close on the father. He's not looking at the view. He's watching her. This was the whole point.

14

Aerial pullback. The Traveler traces a switchback road as the last light goes. They'll remember this one.
